Thraax Sunrise Scramble

Ingredients:

  • 4 Thraax Eggs
  • 4 Thraax Sausages
  • 1 cup of Zorbon Sauce
  • 1 tsp. of Gornian Spice Powder
  • 1/2 cup of Valerian Cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup of Hestorian Broth
  • 1/2 cup of Eltarian Herbs, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, cook Thraax Sausages until browned and crispy, then slice into rounds.
  2. In a separate pan, scramble Thraax Eggs with Hestorian Broth and Eltarian Herbs until fluffy.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the Zorbon Sauce and Gornian Spice Powder to create the "intergalactic hollandaise".
  4. On a plate, arrange the scrambled eggs, sausage rounds, and a generous drizzle of the "intergalactic hollandaise".
  5. Sprinkle with Valerian Cheese and serve with a side of your choice, or with a lighthearted joke like, "Why did the Thraax cross the intergalactic highway? To get to the breakfast buffet, of course!"
  6. Enjoy your hilarious and delicious breakfast from another planet!
